Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions you may care about; if you have other questions, feel free to contact us

How are agricultural products delivered? What areas are covered by delivery?

We have strategic partnerships with SF Express and JD Logistics. All agricultural products are delivered using cold chain preservation to ensure fresh arrival. Delivery covers most provinces and cities nationwide (excluding remote areas like Xinjiang, Tibet, Qinghai, etc.). Delivery usually takes 24-48 hours after placing the order, or 3-5 days for remote areas. Orders over ¥200 qualify for free delivery; otherwise, a ¥15 delivery fee is charged.

How far in advance do I need to book the rural experience? What is the maximum number of people?

We recommend booking the rural experience 3-7 days in advance, and 15 days in advance for weekends and holidays to avoid full capacity. There is no minimum number of people for individual and family experiences (1 person can book). Group team building (10+ people) requires contacting customer service in advance for a customized plan, with a maximum capacity of 200 people at once. Upon successful booking, you will receive a confirmation text message with the detailed address, transportation routes, and important notes.

Can agricultural products be returned or exchanged? What is the return and exchange policy?

We commit to unconditional returns or exchanges for agricultural products with quality issues. If you find quality issues such as spoilage, deterioration, or damage within 24 hours of receiving the goods, please take photos and contact customer service. We will resend or refund your money free of charge. For non-quality issues (such as personal taste preference, ordering error, etc.), returns are supported within 7 days, provided the product is unopened and does not affect secondary sales. Return shipping costs are borne by the customer.

What activities can be customized for group team building? Is catering and accommodation provided?

Group team building activities can be customized based on your needs, including farming experience, fruit/vegetable picking, outdoor BBQ, rural sports games, and folk handicraft classes. We provide a one-stop service, including catering (farm-to-table dishes, self-service BBQ, etc.) and accommodation (rural homestays, characteristic log cabins, etc.), which can be flexibly matched according to team size and budget. We recommend contacting customer service to discuss the customized plan 15 days in advance. Our investment manager will provide a detailed quote and itinerary.

How can I become an agent or partner of Jianxian Agricultural Products?

We welcome capable individuals or companies with resources to become our agents or partners. Cooperation methods include product agency, channel cooperation, and rural experience base franchising. If you are interested in cooperation, please submit your application through the contact form, or call our business cooperation hotline directly: 400-888-9999 ext. 8888. Our investment manager will contact you within 3 business days to discuss cooperation details.
