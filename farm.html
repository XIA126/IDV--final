Our Planting Bases:
Naturally Nurtured, Carefully Cultivated

Jianxian Ag Products currently owns three core planting bases, located in the Northeast Black Soil, Yunnan Alpine, and Shandong Yellow River Delta, with a total area exceeding 2000 mu, forming a modern ecological agricultural base integrating planting, research, processing, and distribution.

We always adhere to the planting philosophy of "Natural, Organic, and Healthy," refusing to use chemical pesticides, fertilizers, and ripening agents. We combine traditional farming methods with modern ecological agriculture technology to maximize the preservation of crops' natural quality and nutritional content.

The bases are far from urban pollution, possessing unique natural conditions: the Northeast base's black soil layer is 30-100 cm thick, rich in humus; the Yunnan base is over 2000 meters above sea level, with ample sunshine and a large day-night temperature difference; the Shandong base is located at the Yellow River estuary, with fertile soil and pure water sources.

Core Base · Northeast

Northeast Black Soil Planting Base

Located in Wuchang City, Heilongjiang Province, situated in the heart of the Songnen Plain, it is one of the world's three major black soil regions. The black soil layer here is 30-100 cm thick, rich in humus and various minerals, with extremely high soil fertility, known as the "Panda of Cultivated Land."

The base covers an area of 1200 mu, mainly planting organic rice, black beans, soybeans, and other grain crops. Thanks to the temperate continental monsoon climate, the summer here is warm and humid, with ample sunshine, and rain and heat occur simultaneously, which is very suitable for grain growth.

We adopt the "rice-duck co-culture" ecological planting model, where ducks forage for pests and weeds in the rice fields, and their droppings become natural fertilizer, forming a virtuous ecological cycle. The rice is harvested manually and naturally air-dried after ripening, preserving the most original rice aroma and nutrition.

Core Base · Yunnan

Yunnan Alpine Ecological Farm

Located in Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province, at an altitude of 2000-2500 meters, it features an alpine three-dimensional climate. It is far from industrial pollution, with fresh air, excellent water quality, and soil rich in trace elements like selenium, making it an ideal growing environment for organic crops.

The base covers an area of 600 mu, mainly planting characteristic crops such as blueberries, apples, walnuts, and cashews. Due to the high altitude, the day-night temperature difference can exceed $15^\circ\text{C}$, which is conducive to the accumulation of sugar and nutrients in the crops, resulting in better fruit taste and richer nutrition.

We adhere to the "following nature" planting philosophy, using natural farming methods, avoiding any chemical agents, relying on manual weeding, physical pest control, and organic fertilizer application. All crops are naturally ripened, without ripening agents or waxing, preserving the most authentic flavor.

Main Crops

  • Yunnan Apples
  • Organic Cashews
  • Alpine Walnuts

Natural Advantages

Core Base · Shandong

Shandong Organic Vegetable Base

Located in Dongying City, Shandong Province, in the Yellow River Delta, a plain formed by the Yellow River estuary alluvial. The soil is fertile, rich in minerals and organic matter. The base covers an area of 800 mu, utilizing a combination of modern greenhouses and open-field cultivation, mainly planting over 20 types of vegetables such as organic lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, broccoli, and carrots. Organic Cultivation Process

From seed selection to harvest, every step adheres to nature and craftsmanship

1

Select Fine Seeds

Selecting high-quality varieties suitable for local climate and soil conditions, prioritizing non-GMO, stress-tolerant traditional varieties. All seeds undergo strict screening and germination testing, ensuring a germination rate of over 95%.

2

Soil Improvement

Improving soil fertility and breathability by returning crop straw to the field, planting green manure, and applying organic fertilizer. Soil is tested before planting, and soil pH and nutrient content are adjusted according to the results to create the best conditions for crop growth.

3

Ecological Farming

Adopting planting models such as rotation, intercropping, and relay cropping to build a diversified farmland ecosystem. Weeding is done manually, rejecting herbicides; physical pest control (insect nets, lure lamps) and biological control (natural enemies) are used, rejecting chemical pesticides.

4

Manual Tending

Manual tending throughout the entire process, regularly inspecting crop growth and promptly removing diseased or weak plants. Using water-saving irrigation methods like drip and spray irrigation, precisely supplying water according to crop needs at different growth stages to avoid water waste.

5

Natural Maturation

Rejecting chemical agents like ripening agents and expanders, following the crop's natural growth cycle, and only harvesting when the fruit is fully ripe. After passing maturity tests, the optimal picking time is selected based on the crop's characteristics to ensure peak taste and nutrition.

Organic Certification

All planting bases have obtained national organic product certification, strictly adhering to organic agricultural production standards, without using chemical pesticides, fertilizers, hormones, or other synthetic substances throughout the process from planting to harvest.

Full Traceability
