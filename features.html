Rural Characteristics · A Symphony of Nature and Culture

Here there is no complicated decoration, only the most authentic rural landscape. Every piece of scenery, every culture, and every experience stems from the gifts of nature and the precipitation of time.

Mountain & Water Sanctuary · Natural Oxygen Bar

The countryside is nestled among rolling mountains, with a forest coverage rate of up to 75%. Three natural streams run through the village, and the negative oxygen ion content far exceeds urban standards, making it a genuine "natural oxygen bar." There is no industrial pollution here, only fresh air, lush green forests, and clear streams.

The scenery changes with the seasons: mountains full of wildflowers in spring, dense shade in summer, layers of dyed forest in autumn, and silver-coated landscapes in winter. Millennium ancient trees, natural waterfalls, and mountain caves are scattered throughout, making every step a scenic view.

Folk Heritage · Cultural Life

The century-old village has accumulated profound folk culture, passed down through generations of villagers, perfectly preserving ancient crafts and customs. There is no elaborate packaging, only the purest atmosphere of life.

Traditional crafts such as bamboo weaving, wood carving, and hand-woven cloth continue here. Festive activities like dragon and lion dances during the Spring Festival, making Zongzi during the Dragon Boat Festival, and moon viewing during the Mid-Autumn Festival are lively and colorful. Rural cuisine, such as firewood-cooked rice, handmade rice cakes, preserved meat, and sausages, are made with original ecological ingredients, pure in taste, carrying the memories of a generation.

Joy of Farming · Return to Authenticity

Say goodbye to superficial sightseeing and deeply participate in rural farming life. Follow local farmers to the fields for plowing, watering, fertilizing, and harvesting, experiencing the entire process from sowing to harvest and appreciating the hard-won nature of food.

In the organic fruit and vegetable greenhouses, fresh produce like strawberries, tomatoes, and cucumbers can be picked and eaten on the spot. In the fields, learn traditional farming techniques and understand the growth cycle of crops. In the farmyard, experience the fun of cooking with firewood and taste the rural delicacies you made yourself.

Homestay Retreat · Quietly Enjoy Time

Stay in an authentic rural homestay, without standardized hotel amenities, only the warmth of home. Wooden structure houses, sunlit courtyards, and starlit nights will let you escape the urban noise.

Wake up to bird song in the morning, open the window to green mountains and clear waters. Sit in the courtyard in the evening, listen to the chirping of insects and frogs, drink a cup of homemade rice wine, and feel the tranquility and comfort of the countryside. The homestay hosts are warm and welcoming, ready to tell you stories of the countryside and recommend off-the-beaten-path travel routes.
